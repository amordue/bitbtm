# Robot Archetype Fallback Assets

These files are organizer-supplied fallback illustrations for robots that do not have an uploaded or imported photo. They are example art only and should never replace a real robot image when one is available.

## Asset Contract

- Format: `svg`
- Dimensions: `1200x900`
- Aspect ratio: `4:3`
- Visual style: clean technical blueprint illustration with crisp drafting lines and restrained panel shading
- View language: primarily side, or top-down blueprint views chosen for the clearest read of the weapon system
- Rendering language: white or pale cyan linework, construction lines, dimension-style accents, sectional hints, and subtle schematic glow
- Background treatment: deep navy or blueprint-blue field with faint grid texture and minimal vignette
- Content rules: no logos, no labels, no captions, no arena scenery, no battle action, no humans, card-friendly composition

## Reusable Prompt / Style Sheet

Base prompt:

> Technical blueprint illustration of a combat robot, clean orthographic-meets-concept-art drafting style, crisp pale cyan and white linework on a deep blueprint-blue background, subtle grid, fine construction marks, restrained schematic shading, readable silhouette, high contrast, no text, no labels, no logos, no arena, no people, composed for a landscape 4:3 card.

Per-archetype addendum:

- `flipper`: low wedge chassis with a front-hinged launcher and broad lifting spatula
- `vertical-spinner`: compact body with a large exposed vertical disc dominating the side profile
- `horizontal-spinner`: low broad chassis with a long weapon bar spanning across the body
- `drum-spinner`: compact rectangular chassis with a prominent front-mounted cylindrical drum
- `hammer`: low wedge body with a tall articulated hammer arm and dense striking head
- `saw`: forward-reaching armature carrying a prominent circular blade
- `lifter`: long front forks with visible support structure and wedge body behind
- `grabber`: crusher-style robot with upper and lower compression arms at the nose
- `cluster`: three separate but visually related mini-bots staged together as one system
- `rammer`: aggressive wedge-first pusher with a reinforced front plough and thick armor geometry
- `generic`: balanced all-purpose combat robot silhouette with no dominant weapon

Negative prompt:

> photoreal render, studio photo, arena crowd, sparks, explosions, battle damage, captions, watermarks, text overlays, humans, branding, excessive clutter, cartoon style, hand-drawn doodles, illegible annotations, poster layout

## Blueprint Style Notes

- Favor 1-2 clear views over busy multi-panel layouts.
- Keep the robot centered and large enough to read well when cropped into UI cards.
- Use dimension-like ticks, sectional callout shapes, or ghosted construction circles only as atmosphere, not as readable text.
- Top-down views work especially well for wide weapon systems such as horizontal bars, clusters, and fork-heavy lifters.
- Preserve a single dominant silhouette per archetype so fallback art remains scannable at small sizes.

## Usage Note

These assets are intended for display fallback only. Uploaded images and sheet-provided robot photos remain the highest-priority image source.
